About InAmigos Foundation

A Section 8 non-profit organization committed to education, environment, animal welfare, and community development since 2020.

Our Story

InAmigos Foundation was founded on 23rd September 2020 in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, by a group of young professionals who believed in the power of collective action. What started as a small initiative to feed the hungry during the pandemic quickly evolved into a registered Section 8 non-profit organization.

Today, we run six flagship projects — Seva, Udaan, Bachpanshala, Prakriti, Jeev, and Vikas — each targeting a critical social need. From distributing over 50,000 meals to planting 20,000+ trees and training 30,000+ interns, our work is rooted in real impact and community participation.

We are guided by the principles of transparency, grassroots action, and sustainable development. Every rupee donated and every hour volunteered goes directly toward creating tangible change on the ground.

What We Stand For

Compassion

We act with empathy, ensuring dignity and care in every service we provide — from meals to medical aid.

Inclusivity

Our programs are designed for all, regardless of background. We believe change must reach the last mile.

Sustainability

Environmental conservation and long-term community resilience are at the core of Project Prakriti and beyond.

Integrity

We maintain complete transparency in operations and finances, holding 80G, 12A, CSR-1, and ISO 9001:2015 certifications.

Our Journey

2020
Foundation & Emergency Relief: Registered as a Section 8 company. Launched Project Seva, distributing cooked meals and dry rations to migrant families and daily wage workers during COVID-19 lockdowns.
2021
Expansion into Education: Initiated Project Bachpanshala to support underprivileged children with study materials, mentorship, and weekend learning camps in rural Bilaspur.
2022
Environmental & Youth Focus: Launched Project Prakriti (tree plantation drives) and Project Udaan (skill workshops). Received 80G and 12A tax exemption status.
2023
Animal Welfare & Internships: Started Project Jeev for stray animal feeding and rescue. Project Vikas began offering structured internships, training over 15,000 students.
2024
National Recognition: Crossed 50,000 meals distributed and 20,000 trees planted. Received ISO 9001:2015 certification and CSR-1 registration, enabling corporate partnerships.
