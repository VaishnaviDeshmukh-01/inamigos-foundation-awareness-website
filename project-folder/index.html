WHO WE ARE

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organization founded on 23rd September 2020 in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We are committed to building a better society by working in the areas of education, environment, animal welfare, and community development.

Our Mission

Empower communities through sustainable initiatives and create opportunities for a better life.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has equal opportunity to grow, learn and live with dignity.
